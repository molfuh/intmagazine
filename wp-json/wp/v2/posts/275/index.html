Amber Goldhammer is an exuberant artist who uses bright colors and love motifs in her
inspirational graffiti work. Her artistic talents have always been there, but before an
injury pushed her into mixed medium physical art, she was a performance artist in
theatre and gymnastics.<\/p>\n\n\n\n


Goldhammer was born and raised in Portland, where her creative Grandmother inspired
her. Into her adulthood she moved to Los Angeles. \u201cI studied under master artist and
mentor, Rassouli, the founder of the Fusionart movement that aims to marry Eastern
mysticism with Western art techniques.\u201d She learned to follow what is in her heart rather
than what she sees.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
