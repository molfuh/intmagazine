While there is no shortage of luxury boutiques in the world to spend your money at,
there is one that stands out from the rest for a number of reasons: Wheat Boutique.
Wheat Boutique was founded by Molly Shaheen and Huw Collins, a young power
couple with a dream to open up their own boutique. The two come from very different
backgrounds, with Collins being a former star on the show \u201cPretty Little Liars\u201d and
Shaheen being the daughter of a U.S Senator, but they both share a love for fashion
that ultimately inspired them to create Wheat Boutique.<\/p>\n\n\n\n


The brand\u2019s namesake came about as a result of a trip to Paris in 2016 upon which
Shaheen and Collins visited Coco Chanel\u2019s former apartment. On the tour, Shaheen
couldn\u2019t help but notice all the motifs and symbols of wheat throughout the apartment.
Later, she learned of Chanel\u2019s strong belief in wheat\u2019s power of prosperity, and
Shaheen knew it was something she wanted to use as inspiration in her own life. Thus,
Wheat Boutique was born.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
